San Francisco & Bay Area Freethinkers: Atheist, Agnostic & Others - Meetings of Alcoholics Anonymous

Several ways to attend Zoom meetings: computer, smartphone, telephone. All ways connect with the same meeting.

OPTION 1: Video & Audio-Only via computer or smartphone

The first time you connect, you will be prompted to install a piece of software, the Zoom app. It's necessary in order to participate via computer or smartphone (but: see below for dial-in options).

And the first time you join a meeting, you may be asked to register. It'll be just that once, per meeting, and it helps keep out the zoom bombers and internet trolls.
